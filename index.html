about omar

I am a: designer, technologist, and humanist. At work, I manage and design care-focused applications at Arcadia.io. The products I work on empower clinicians to manage patients' social and care needs.

Since graduating with a Biomedical Engineering degree in 2015, I have been drawn to creative, socially-minded applications of technology. In 2016, I moved from a data engineering position to product management in pursuit of a more craft-based, human-centered role.

Outside of work, I have developed a deep fascination for the interplay between data, technology, and the human experience. This curiousity has led me to pursue a part-time degree in Data Visualization at Parsons School of Design (2020 - 2022).
