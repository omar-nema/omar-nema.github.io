Using d3.js, I visualized patient survey data for the Medicare/Medicaid population of over two thousand hospitals. Each hospital 'thread' below weaves along a path based on four key factors: size, patient experience, clinical outcomes, and cost. Collectively, the threads visualize the complex, textured relationship between these four patterns.
